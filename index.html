About Me

I'm Haris — a game designer, developer, UX researcher, and evaluator with a Bachelor's of Arts in Games and Interactive Media (minor in Game Design and Development) from Michigan State University. I'm currently halfway through my Master's of Science in User Experience Design, also at MSU.

My background spans both sides of the design-development divide. On the game side, I've shipped projects in cross-disciplinary teams, owning UI/UX from Figma wireframe to Unity implementation. On the research side, I conduct usability studies, heuristic evaluations, journey mapping, and persona work — applying the same rigor to digital products that I bring to game interfaces.

I'm broadly computer savvy and thrive in inter-disciplinary environments where designers, developers, and researchers need to speak the same language. Whether a project calls for a polished HUD, a research report, or a working prototype, I'm comfortable across the stack.
