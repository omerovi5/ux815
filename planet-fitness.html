Planet Fitness Research Project

UX Research Figma Usability Study UI Redesign

Overview

A comprehensive UX research and redesign project focused on the Planet Fitness mobile application. The goal was to identify usability pain points through structured research methods, then propose and prototype solutions using Figma.

Research Methods

  • User interviews with Planet Fitness members of varying experience levels
  • Persona mapping based on interview findings
  • User journey mapping to identify friction points in the app flow
  • System blueprinting to understand backend touchpoints affecting UX
  • Usability and accessibility evaluation using heuristic analysis
  • Written research report with findings and recommendations

Outcome

Findings were synthesized into a high-fidelity Figma prototype addressing the top three pain points discovered during evaluation: confusing class booking flow, poor visual hierarchy on the home screen, and inaccessible typography throughout the app.

Tools Used

  • Figma (wireframing, prototyping, component design)
  • User interview facilitation
  • Journey mapping & persona creation
  • Written research report (Google Docs)
